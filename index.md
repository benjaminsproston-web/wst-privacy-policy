Weight Strength Tracker (WST) Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 June 2026

Thank you for using Weight Strength Tracker (WST). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app stores, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Store

Weight Strength Tracker is designed as an offline-first fitness application.

The app may store the following information on your device:

Workout sessions
Exercise names
Sets, repetitions and weights
Personal records
Estimated one-repetition maximum (1RM) calculations
Custom exercises
Exercise images selected by the user
AI coaching insights generated within the app
Premium subscription status
App preferences and settings

This information is stored locally on your device unless you choose to export a backup.

2. Backups

WST allows users to create manual backup files.

Backup files:

are generated only when requested by the user;
are stored in a location chosen by the user;
are never uploaded to our servers.

When restoring a backup, the selected file is read only to restore your workout data into the application.

3. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, WST may send notifications including:

inactivity reminders;
monthly training report reminders;
premium feature reminders;
future workout reminders.

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

4. Premium Subscription

WST offers optional premium features through Google Play Subscriptions.

Subscription payments are processed entirely by Google Play.

WST does not collect, process, or store:

payment card information;
bank details;
billing information.

Subscription status is provided securely through Google Play Billing.

5. Data Sharing

WST does not sell, rent, or share your workout data with third parties.

Your training information remains stored on your device unless you choose to export a backup file.

6. Third-Party Services

WST may use the following Google services:

Google Play Billing
Google Play Services
Android Notification Services

These services operate under Google's own Privacy Policy.

7. Advertising

Weight Strength Tracker contains no advertisements.

8. Children's Privacy

WST is not specifically directed towards children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Security

Your workout information is stored locally on your device.

We recommend protecting your device using a screen lock and creating regular backups of your workout data.

10. Your Rights

You may:

delete your workout history at any time;
uninstall the application;
delete exported backup files;
restore backup files that you have previously created.
11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date shown at the top of this document.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 June 2026

Thank you for using Weight Strength Tracker (WST). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app stores, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Store

Weight Strength Tracker is designed as an offline-first fitness application. The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Workout sessions
  • Exercise names
  • Sets, repetitions and weights
  • Personal records
  • Estimated one-repetition maximum calculations
  • Custom exercises
  • Exercise images selected by the user
  • Locally generated coaching insights
  • Premium subscription status
  • App preferences and settings

2. Backups

WST allows users to create backup files. Backup files are generated only when requested by the user, are stored in a location chosen by the user, and are not uploaded to our servers. When restoring a backup, the selected file is read only to restore your workout data into the application.

3. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, WST may send notifications such as inactivity reminders, monthly training report reminders, premium feature reminders, and future workout reminders. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

4. Premium Subscription

WST may offer optional premium features through Google Play subscriptions. Subscription payments are processed by Google Play. WST does not collect, process, or store payment card information, bank details, or billing information. Subscription status may be provided by Google Play Billing to determine whether premium features should be unlocked.

5. Data Sharing

WST does not sell, rent, or share your personal workout data with third parties. Your training information remains stored on your device unless you choose to export a backup file.

6. Third-Party Services

WST may use Google Play Billing, Google Play Services, and Android notification services. These services operate under Google's own privacy policies and terms.

7. Advertising

Weight Strength Tracker contains no advertising.

8. Children's Privacy

WST is not specifically directed towards children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Security

Your workout information is stored locally on your device. We recommend protecting your device with a screen lock and creating regular backups of your workout data.

10. Your Rights

You may delete your workout history, uninstall the application, delete exported backup files, and restore backup files you have previously created.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date shown at the top of this document.
